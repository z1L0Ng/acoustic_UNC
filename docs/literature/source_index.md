## Extraction Checklist

- What clinical problem does the paper actually study?
- What audio signal is used, and how was it captured?
- What labels or reference standards are used?
- Is the dataset public, private, clinical, crowdsourced, or simulated?
- What preprocessing and features are used?
- What baselines and model families are compared?
- Which metrics are reported, and on what split?
- What failure modes, bias risks, or deployment constraints are mentioned?
- Does the paper support a feasible preliminary experiment for this project?
